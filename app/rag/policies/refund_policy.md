# Refund & Return Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** June 1, 2025  
**Policy ID:** POL-REFUND-001  
**Version:** 3.2

---

## 1. Overview

ShopSmart Inc. ("we", "us", "our") is committed to customer satisfaction. This policy outlines the terms and conditions under which customers may return purchased items and receive refunds. All customer-support agents must adhere strictly to the guidelines in this document when processing refund and return requests.

---

## 2. General Return Window

| Category | Return Window | Condition |
|---|---|---|
| General merchandise | 30 calendar days from delivery | Unused, original packaging |
| Electronics & appliances | 14 calendar days from delivery | Unopened, sealed packaging |
| Clothing & apparel | 30 calendar days from delivery | Unworn, tags attached |
| Furniture & large items | 14 calendar days from delivery | Unassembled, original packaging |
| Subscription boxes | 7 calendar days from delivery | Unopened |

The return window begins on the **date of delivery** as confirmed by the carrier's tracking information, not the date of purchase.

---

## 3. Condition Requirements

To be eligible for a full refund, returned items **must** meet all of the following criteria:

1. **Unused / Unopened:** The item must not have been used, worn, installed, or consumed.  
2. **Original Packaging:** The item must be returned in its original manufacturer packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and inserts.  
3. **Tags & Seals Intact:** For clothing and electronics, all original tags, security seals, and shrink-wrap must be intact.  
4. **No Physical Damage:** The item must be free of scratches, dents, stains, or any other damage not present at time of delivery.  
5. **Proof of Purchase:** Customer must provide a valid order number, receipt, or email confirmation.

Items that do not meet these conditions may be eligible for a **partial refund** (see Section 6).

---

## 4. Refund Methods

Refunds are processed using the **original payment method** by default:

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Credit / debit card | 5–10 business days | Depends on issuing bank |
| PayPal | 3–5 business days | Returned to PayPal balance |
| Store credit / gift card | 1–2 business days | Issued as new store-credit code |
| Bank transfer / ACH | 7–14 business days | Requires account verification |
| Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) | 5–10 business days | Refund applied to BNPL balance |

### Store Credit Option

Customers may elect to receive a **store-credit refund** instead of returning funds to their original payment method. Store-credit refunds are processed within **24 hours** and include a **10% bonus** (e.g., a $50 refund becomes $55 in store credit). Store credits do not expire.

---

## 5. Non-Refundable Items

The following categories are **not eligible** for return or refund under any circumstances:

- **Gift cards and e-gift certificates**
- **Digital downloads** (software, e-books, music, streaming codes)
- **Perishable goods** (food, flowers, live plants)
- **Personal-care and hygiene products** (opened cosmetics, skincare, undergarments)
- **Customised / personalised items** (engraved products, monogrammed goods, custom prints)
- **Clearance / final-sale items** (marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase)
- **Hazardous materials** (flammable liquids, aerosols, chemicals)
- **Items purchased with a promotional discount greater than 50%** (unless defective)

> **Agent Note:** If a customer received a defective or wrong item in a non-refundable category, escalate to a supervisor. Defective products are always eligible for refund or replacement regardless of category.

---

## 6. Partial Refund Rules

A **partial refund** may be issued when the item does not meet full-return conditions but is still returnable:

| Condition | Refund Percentage |
|---|---|
| Opened but unused, packaging intact | 85% of purchase price |
| Opened and lightly used (< 7 days of use) | 70% of purchase price |
| Missing accessories or manuals | 75% of purchase price |
| Damaged packaging but item intact | 90% of purchase price |
| Item returned outside return window (within 45 days) | 50% of purchase price (store credit only) |

Partial refunds are calculated on the **item price at time of purchase**, excluding shipping costs and taxes. Taxes are refunded proportionally.

---

## 7. Shipping Cost Policies

### Customer-Initiated Returns

- Customers are responsible for **return shipping costs** unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective product, shipping damage).
- A prepaid return label may be provided; the cost ($5.99 for standard, $9.99 for expedited) is deducted from the refund.

### ShopSmart-Fault Returns

If the return is due to **our error**, we cover all shipping costs:

- Wrong item shipped
- Defective / damaged on arrival
- Item not matching product description
- Missing items from order

In these cases, the customer receives a **free prepaid return label** and the full refund includes original shipping charges.

### Free Shipping Threshold

If a customer received **free shipping** on an order exceeding $75 and returns items that bring the order total below $75, the standard shipping fee ($7.99) is deducted from the refund.

---

## 8. Holiday Extended Returns

During the holiday season, an extended return window applies:

- **Purchases made between November 1 and December 31** may be returned through **January 31 of the following year**.
- All standard conditions (Section 3) still apply.
- Electronics retain the standard **14-day window** from delivery, with no holiday extension.
- Clearance and final-sale exclusions remain in effect.

---

## 9. Processing Timelines

| Step | Timeline |
|---|---|
| Return request approval | Within 24 hours of submission |
| Return label issuance | Immediately upon approval |
| Refund initiation (after item received) | 1–3 business days |
| Refund visible in customer account | 5–10 business days from initiation |
| Store-credit issuance | Within 24 hours of approval |

### Quality Inspection

All returned items undergo a **quality inspection** upon receipt at our warehouse. If the item condition does not match the customer's description, the refund amount may be adjusted downward per the partial-refund schedule (Section 6). The customer is notified via email of any adjustments.

---

## 10. Exchanges

- Exchanges are processed as a **refund + new order**.
- The customer returns the original item and places a new order for the desired item.
- If an even exchange of the same item in a different size or colour is requested, we waive return shipping.

---

## 11. Order Cancellations

- Orders may be cancelled for a full refund if they have **not yet shipped**.
- Once an order has shipped, the customer must follow the standard return process.
- Pre-orders can be cancelled at any time before the item ships.

---

## 12. Dispute Resolution

If a customer disputes a refund decision:

1. The agent re-reviews the case against this policy.
2. If the customer remains unsatisfied, escalate to a **Team Lead** within 4 hours.
3. Team Leads may authorise exceptions up to **$50 in goodwill credit**.
4. Disputes involving more than $200 require **Manager** approval.
5. If no resolution is reached, the customer may file a formal complaint through our Customer Advocacy team.

---

## 13. Fraud Prevention

Refund requests are subject to automated fraud screening. See the separate **Fraud Detection Policy (POL-FRAUD-001)** for detailed rules. Key thresholds:

- More than **3 refund requests in 30 days** triggers a manual review.
- Refund amount exceeding **80% of order value** is flagged for verification.
- Accounts less than **30 days old** with refund requests are flagged.
